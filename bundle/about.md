# Hours

Second Home operates in three modes across the day/week:

* **Cafe**: Wednesday–Sunday, 9:00–17:00. See the [cafe menu](/cafe/).
* **Bar**: 17:00–23:00.
* **Event space** — available for private bookings and hosted events; see [Global Friends in Nagoya](#global-friends-in-nagoya) below and the [full events list](/events/) for the current lineup.
* Closed: Monday, Tuesday

# About

Second Home is a basement-level venue entered through a door disguised as a painting. It currently functions as a cafe by day, a bar by evening, and an event space on select days. Reviewers describe it as a whimsical "hidden gem" hideout with secret/private rooms and a playful, stylish, movie-like atmosphere. It was featured on TV shortly after opening. Live piano performances are held on weekends — see [Weekend Piano](/events/weekend-piano.md).

# History

* Originally, Second Home was just a rented space where the [Global Friends in Nagoya](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/) community organized events — there was no bar or cafe.
* The space later began operating as a bar in the evenings.
* Most recently, it also started operating as a cafe during the day. The Tabelog cafe listing shows an opening date of February 28, 2026 for this daytime cafe service — that date marks the start of the cafe, not the founding of the venue itself.
* The cafe operates independently from Global Friends in Nagoya — it is a separate business, not a Global Friends activity. The two share the same owners, which is why they share the same physical space.
