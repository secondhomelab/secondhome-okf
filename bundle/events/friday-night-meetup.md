A social event at [Second Home](/about.md), organized under the [Global Friends in Nagoya](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/) Meetup group, on the Fridays when the externally-organized [Cosmo International Party](cosmo-international-party.md) doesn't run (Cosmo takes Fridays every other week).

* Day: Friday, every other week (alternating with Cosmo International Party)
* Time: 19:00–24:00

# Example Themes

On these alternate Fridays, the theme is likely [Board Games](board-games.md) or Karaoke. *Check [Global Friends on Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/events/) for the current lineup.*
