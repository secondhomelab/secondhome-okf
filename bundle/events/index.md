# Global Friends in Nagoya events

Events at Second Home are, for practical purposes, [Global Friends in Nagoya](/about.md#global-friends-in-nagoya) events. Each weekly slot below rotates through different themed nights (e.g. Burger Party, K-Pop Night) rather than always running under the same name — see each event's "Example Themes" section, or check [Global Friends on Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/events/) for the current schedule.

* [Board Games](board-games.md) - Weekly, Saturday early evening 17:00–19:00.
* [International Party](international-party.md) - Recurring international-themed party, Saturday evenings 19:00–24:00.
* [International Midweek Mixer](midweek-mixer.md) - Weekly, Wednesday evenings 19:00–23:00.
* [Friday Night Meetup](friday-night-meetup.md) - Every other Friday, 19:00–24:00 (alternates with Cosmo International Party).
* [Quiz Night](quiz-night.md) - Monthly.
* [DJ Freestyle Meetup](dj-freestyle.md) - Monthly, Sundays.

# Externally-organized events

Independent communities that also host events at Second Home, separate from Global Friends in Nagoya.

* [Cosmo International Party](cosmo-international-party.md) - Hosted by the Cosmo International Party Nagoya Meetup group — Sundays, and Fridays every two weeks.
* [Aichi Connection](https://www.facebook.com/AichiConnection/) also hosts or co-hosts events at Second Home.
