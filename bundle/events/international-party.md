A recurring event held at [Second Home](/about.md) during its event-space hours, organized under the [Global Friends in Nagoya](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/) Meetup group. Unlike the [Cosmo International Party](cosmo-international-party.md), which is run by an external group, this event is managed directly by the Second Home owners. It follows directly after [Board Games](board-games.md) on the same evening.

* Day: Saturday
* Time: 19:00–24:00

# Example Themes

This Saturday slot rotates through different themed nights rather than always running under the same name. "International Party" is the general/default theme; observed variations include:

* Burger Party (seen Saturday, July 11, 2026)

*Check [Global Friends on Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/global-friends/events/) for the current lineup.*
